Famous Cats

Garfield

The comic Garfield was created by Jim Davis. It has been in publication since 1978 and has been run in over 2500 different publications. Garfield has been featured in many books, movies and animated series as well.


Cat in the Hat

The Cat in the Hat was written by Dr Seuss in 1957. It is one of the most popular early books for children learning how to read. Multiple movies and plays have been adapted from this book.

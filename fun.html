Fun Facts

Cats are the most popular pet in the United States. A group of cats is called a clowder. Cats have over 20 muscles to control their ears. Cats will sleep 70% of their life. Stubbs the cat was the mayor of Talkeetna, Alaska.


Adult cats only meow to communicate with humans. A cat can jump up to 6 times its length. Cats use whiskers to determine if they can fit into a space. Cats only sweat through their foot pads.

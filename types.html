Types

There are over 70 different breeds of cats, but we are going to focus on the top 3.


Persian: The persian is the most popular breed in the US. This is a long haired cat that is very affectionate and loyal. They are characterized by a round face and short muzzle.


Maine Coon: The maine coon is a larger size house cat that is known to be very gentle and loving. Hence the nickname "The Gentle Giant". They have been known to average from 15 to 25 pounds. Their size makes them take longer to mature, 3 years vs the average one year for most cats.


Exotic: The exotic is a lot like the persian, with the main exception that it has short hair. This signifigantly cuts down on the amount of grooming needed

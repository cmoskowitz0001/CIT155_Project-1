Care

There are many elements to caring for a cat. Feeding, Grooming, and Litter training, are some of the most important factors.

Feeding

Cats need a balanced diet the same as any animal. Most vets will recommend which foods are necessary. Cats also need clean water at all times, so make sure they have a fresh bowl daily.



Grooming

Most cats bathe theselves and rarely need a bath. But the occassional bath can keep their coats clean and shiny. Brushing and removing access hair helps maintain sheeding and will keep your pets area clean. This also reduces the amount of hairballs that occur.


Litter Training

All indoor cats need a litter box. Make sure it is always accessible and clean. Scoop daily and wash the box out with soap once a week. Stay away from ammonia and scented products when cleaning the litter box.

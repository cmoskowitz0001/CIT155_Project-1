Meme Cats

Nyan Cat

8-bit animated cat, often referred to as poptart rainbow cat, has been on the internet since Aprill 2011. There are multiple video games and Youtube videos using this cat-like character.


Chemistry Cat

Chemistry cat is used to make science pun related humor. This meme comes and goes as needed when only a pun will get the point across.

Grumpy Cat

Born Tardar Sauce on April 4, 2012, Grumpy cat is aptly named due to the permanent look of grumpiness on her face. Reddit is credited for making her famous. She has appeared on News shows, American Idol, TV commercials, the Bachelorette, and WWE Monday Night Raw.
